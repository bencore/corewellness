Meet Core.

Your Personal Meditation Trainer

A Guide for you.

Meditation can be hard. But you don’t have to go it alone. The Core personal meditation trainer recommends meditations for you from its library, helps you stay focused with its pulse, and tracks your progress using biosensors. Core makes meditation easy so you can de-stress, refresh and get back to what you care about.

Focusing pulse.

Core’s vibrating pulse helps you stay grounded and focused. Select from three modes: Guided Breathing, Soothing Backdrop, or Interval Pulse, and tune to taste. When you find yourself thinking about that work deadline or worrying you left the oven on, let Core give you the nudge you need to get back on track. Then maybe double check the oven is off…

Meditation measured.

Looking at your heart is a great way to understand your stress levels and overall health. During each meditation, Core’s biosensors measure Electrocardiogram (EKG), a highly accurate way to view your heart's and nervous system's response to meditation. After your session, you’ll see health metrics important to understanding your meditation, like Heart Rate Variability, Heart Rate, and Calm time.

Designed for everywhere.

No matter where life takes you, Core is ready for the ride. Made with a dark Rosewood, Core’s beautiful design fits seamlessly into the home. And when it’s time to hit the road, Core’s 14 days of battery life makes taking it along a stress free experience.

Technical Specs:

Battery Life: Core's built-in rechargeable battery lasts over 14 days when fully charged.

Sensors: Core's biosensors measure Electrocardiogram (EKG), a highly accurate way to view your heart and nervous system's response to meditation.

Connection: Core uses BLE (Bluetooth Low Energy) to communicate with the app.

Requirements: A compatible smartphone (iOS 9.0+ / Android OS 4.0+).

Support and Warranty: Comes with 1-year limited warranty and customer support for life.
