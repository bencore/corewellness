Senior Product Manager

Full-Time - San Francisco, CA - In-House

At Core, we’re changing the way people approach mental wellbeing. We believe that health starts with mental health – a healthy mind leads to a healthy body and a happier and more fulfilled society.  Our entire team has experienced the incredible benefits that meditation can have, but we know it’s hard to make meditation a part of your everyday routine. The goal of our Product Management team is to design and ship experiences that make meditation easier and more engaging for all of the people who are still trying to establish the habit.

You will be reporting directly to the CEO and responsible for setting direction, defining requirements, and coordinating the team in order to ship meaningful experiences to our customers. This includes deep collaboration across hardware, firmware, mobile, cloud, content, marketing, and customer support. You are the voice of the customer in the eyes of the entire product team, as such we will look to you as the spirit, and the glue, keeping us all moving forward. As we begin to grow beyond meditation, you will help identify other areas where we can contribute to our customers’ greater overall well-being, starting with that foundation of meditation.

At Core, we are grateful to have built an extremely collaborative and close-knit group of talented individuals that care deeply about our mission to bring mental well-being to more people around the world. We have found from first-hand experience that bringing in all possible perspectives to the design and development process is crucial to the success of any product experience. We look for people that thrive in this environment, and can keep the energy alive.

 

Responsibilities

  • Efficiently launch valuable product experiences.
  • Understand the customer circumstance and gather product requirements.
  • Engage closely with customers and translates their feedback into product improvements .
  • Engage closely with designers to define user flows and experiences that are intuitive to use and provide meaningful value.
  • Engage closely with the engineering team to help determine the best technical implementation methods as well as a reasonable implementation schedule.
  • Develop concise product specs that set clear expectations, scoping, and requirements.
  • Define processes and implement tactics that will help our team stay coordinated and on schedule.

Minimum Qualifications

  • 4+ years of product management or related experience.
  • Experience with product management or product design with a focus on software   and/or consumer electronics products.
  • Demonstrated deep user empathy and an eye for high quality design.

 

Preferred Qualifications

  • 6+ years of product management or relevant experience.
  • Excellent communication and organization skills.
  • Laser focus on meaningful outcomes and tangible metrics.
  • Experience building cross-functional products and services at scale.
  • UX design portfolio with relevant experience in consumer electronics or related field
  • Embraces ambiguity, prioritizes efficiently, communicates early, and ultimately gets-it-done.
  • Demonstrated ability to achieve strategic goals in a resource-constrained and fast-paced environment.
